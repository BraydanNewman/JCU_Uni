Goals

The Goal of the Website will be to increase the peoples awareness of the of the cafe and to help new and repeat customers come back to the local cafe. We are also looking to expand our client base to entice more families to the cafe.

Success Evaluation

This is measurable by seeing if the average amount of customers a (day/week/month) increases after the website is launched. This will show a combination of how often customers are returning and new customers are coming to the cafe. We can also evaluate the success by seeing how many customers are signing up for the weekly specials.

Target Audience

As stated in the Introductory email from client PDF the target audience are family's with the people in the the 18 - 35 range with this information. The fact that this is a cafe with the name "sun and moon cafe" some people will find the name makes them feel like it would be a great place to site and enjoy your surroundings at any time of the day or night as the name of the cafe is one that makes you feel happy and peaceful making it a place that family would like to come as they feel that they would be welcome. The website aims to gives a clean and sophisticated appearance, with a easy to operate and navigate forefront. Each preview of the meal allows the chance for young children to see what they are ordering before it gets to the table, making it easier for everyone.
