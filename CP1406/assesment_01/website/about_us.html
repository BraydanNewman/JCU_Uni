About Our Cafe

Sun and Moon Café is locally owned and operated. The Café was proudly established in 2012 and we are located near Central Point Plaza in Bowen. It is a favourite for the laid-back locals and passing tourists. We offer freshly roasted coffee made by our award winning Baristas. Home-style dishes and outstanding customer service is what we pride out selves on.
