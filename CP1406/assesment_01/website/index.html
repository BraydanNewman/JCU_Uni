Sun and Moon Café, located at 223 Powell Street, Bowen offers you a quiet place to sit and relax. One of our award winning baristas or prestigious chefs can deliver you with our freshly made coffee or a mouth-watering home-style meal.

Our aim at Sun and Moon café is to ensure each individual enters a positive and laid-back atmosphere, and feels welcome to sit back and unwind and enjoy all that we have to offer. We are a family friendly, community centred café, offering a play area for young children, while parents have a chance to sit and relax.
